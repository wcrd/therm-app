About

The THERM app has been designed to help contractors, engineers, architects, and students anaylse and document the performance of different constructions for their projects.

Key functionality of the app is summarised below.

Constructions

  • Custom material definitions (thickness & thermal conductivity)
  • Drag & Drop reordering of layers
  • Unventilated air-cavities (up to 300mm)

Calculations

  • Total U & R value
  • Layer R values
  • Surface Resistance (optional)
  • Temperature Gradients

Exporting & Importing

  • Each calculation is recorded in local history
  • User can name individual constructions and reload them from history
  • History can be exported to a CSV file
  • Constructions can be imported from a CSV file
